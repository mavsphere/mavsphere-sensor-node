# Code of Conduct

MavSphere is committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone regardless 
of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, race, 
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and kind
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Sexualised language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces including GitHub issues, pull requests, and discussions.

## Enforcement

**Please note:** MavSphere is maintained by a single developer alongside full-time 
employment. This is not a large organisation with a dedicated moderation team. 
Serious concerns can be reported to conduct@mavsphere.com and will be handled as 
capacity allows. For persistent or serious harassment, GitHub's own 
[reporting tools](https://docs.github.com/en/communities/maintaining-your-safety-on-github/reporting-abuse-or-spam) 
are also available and may be faster.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the 
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1.
